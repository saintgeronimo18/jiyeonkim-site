A little about me

I'm a professional drummer with broad experience in orchestral sessions, film music, and live performances. I've worked as a session drummer for the Seoul Philharmonic Orchestra (Park Concert, 2024), Disney Concert productions (2023), and Yeosu Orchestra (2025). I also participated in film projects including Happy New Year and Sugar, as well as numerous marching band performances across Korea. In addition, I served as a parade leader for the Squid Game parade in Korea and have performed in a wide range of concerts and live gigs. My lessons focus on strong fundamentals, groove, musicality, and real-world playing skills, with guidance tailored to each student's level and goals.
